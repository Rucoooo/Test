FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ProductPro.

How does the 14-day free trial work?

Our 14-day free trial gives you full access to all features of the ProductPro platform. No credit card is required to start your trial. At the end of your trial period, you can choose the plan that best fits your needs.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to the additional features and will be billed the prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

How secure is my data?

We take security seriously. ProductPro uses bank-level encryption (AES-256) for all data, both in transit and at rest. We maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance and conduct regular security audits. Our infrastructure is hosted on AWS with multiple redundancies and geo-distributed backups.

What kind of support is included?

All plans include email support with a guaranteed response time of 24 hours on business days. Professional plans include priority support with response times under 8 hours. Enterprise plans include 24/7 phone support and a dedicated account manager for personalized assistance.
